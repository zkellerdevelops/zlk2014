Taylor Swift Worldwide Livestream

Splash Page

We spent hundreds of hours designing Taylor Swift's 1989 packaging, collateral and branding at ST8MNT, INC. Just before the album launched, she requested a splash page be created to announce a livestream where she debuted her "Shake It Off" single. We were tasked to use the established branding, as to not give away the look of the unreleased 1989 album. We merged this new look with her old without giving too many details away.

Route 91 Harvest Festival

Website

Route 91 Harvest Festival is a yearly country music festival put on by Live Nation in Las Vegas. They tasked us with creating a scalable, responsive website which communicated the festival's brand, while at the same time communicating all things Vegas.

The Trouble With Paris

Book Cover

The Trouble With Paris is a companion book to a video series of the same name by Mark Sayers. The theme is how to find Jesus in a consumer driven modern world. Published by Thomas Nelson Publishers, this cover needed to be appeal to twenty somethings, and reference consumer overload while also focusing on Jesus himself.

PowerNationTV

Responsive Site

PowerNation TV, formerly known as PowerBlock TV, is a custom muscle car and truck DIY television series. The have 4 weekly shows which showcase car builds and upgrades, and parts from well known manufacturers across the automotive industry. Their programs reach a combined 245 million homes, airing on NBC Sports Network, Spike TV and CBS Sports Network. This site needed to be fully responsive, and capable of serving up thousands of hours of on demand episodes. Load times and performance were critical to this site build.

ETSU Arts Initiative

Responsive Site

The ETSU Arts Initiative is a communtiy movement at East Tennessee State University to realize a 30 year dream of funding and building a community performing arts center for Johnson City and the surrounding areas. I am proud to say that the key art, branding, and website we created while I was at ST8MNT, INC won a Gold Addy Award.
