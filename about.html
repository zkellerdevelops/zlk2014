About Me.

Hey There.

My name is Zach Keller. I was born and raised in Nashville, TN and I am proud to say I live here with my wife Jennifer, our son EJ, and our hound dog Knox. I went to school at the University of Tennessee where I earned a BFA in Graphic Design in 2004. I've been working as a designer since then at publishing companies, agencies, and TV production studios.

In 2012 I decided the world of print design was not enough for me, and I left my job to attend the Nashville Software School full-time. There I learned how to take my design knowledge and apply it to the world of front end development. This opened a lot of doors for me that were previously closed. It was one of the best decisions of my career and I have not looked back.

Currently I am working as a Front-End Developer at Centresource Interactive Agency in Nashville's Germantown neighborhood.

I have a love for music, college football, soccer, and cars. Especially Volkswagens. You can find me anywhere there are cool cars and good music.

2015 Zach Keller
